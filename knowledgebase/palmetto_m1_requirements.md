# Palmetto Finance — Solar Energy Plan: Install (M1) Photo Requirements

Source: https://help.palmetto.finance/en/articles/8306274-solar-energy-plan-install-m1-photo-documentation
Last reviewed: April 2026

This document is the authoritative internal reference for what Palmetto requires at M1 submission. It drives checklist generation and compliance scoring. Update this file whenever Palmetto changes requirements — do not rely on tribal knowledge.

---

## How to Use This Knowledgebase

Each requirement has:
- An **ID** (e.g., `PS1`, `R1`, `E1`) — use these as canonical identifiers in checklists
- A **condition** — when it applies (always, if_applicable, or a specific trigger)
- A **description** — what must be in the photo
- **Validation notes** — what makes a photo pass or fail

---

## Section 1: PROJECT SITE

### PS1 — Inverter / Micro-inverter / Optimizer Label
- **Condition:** Required IF monitoring portal access not provided to LightReach at M1
- **Required photo:** Clearly legible model label for inverter, micro-inverter, or AC equipment
- **Validation:** Model name and manufacturer must be readable. Blurry or partial shots fail.

### PS2 — Q.Home / IQ Combiner Label & Serial Number
- **Condition:**
  - MANDATORY for incentive states (regardless of monitoring portal access)
  - Optional in non-incentive states if monitoring portal access is granted
  - If Enphase system with >4 branch circuits and dedicated sub panel: must also photograph Enphase Envoy serial number
- **Required photo:** Clearly legible serial numbers for inverter & DC safety switch (SolarEdge), or Enphase IQ combiner box serial
- **Validation:** Full serial number must be readable

### PS3 — MCI Location & Picture
- **Condition:** Tesla systems ONLY
- **Required photos:**
  1. MCI location shown on a string map
  2. Photo of the MCI installed on site
- **Validation:** Both photos required for Tesla. Not applicable to SolarEdge or Enphase.

### PS4 — Module Label
- **Condition:** Always required
- **Required photo:** Manufacturer label from a panel
- **Validation:** Label must show make/model specs. Must be legible.

### PS5 — Module Serial Number
- **Condition:** Always required
- **Required photo:** Single serial number from one module installed on site
- **Purpose:** O&M tracking, enables manufacturer lot recall identification
- **Validation:** Full serial number must be readable

### PS6 — Pre-Existing Site Damage
- **Condition:** If applicable (pre-existing damage present)
- **Required photo:** Document any pre-existing damage — roof damage, altered satellite dishes, gutters, or other conditions
- **Purpose:** Protects installer from workmanship liability
- **Validation:** Photo must clearly show the existing condition

## Section 2: ROOF / MOUNTING SYSTEM

### R1 — Close-Up of Attachments (Flashing & Sealant)
- **Condition:** Always required
- **Required photos (varies by racking type):**
  - Racking WITH flashing: Photos showing properly installed flashing to manufacturer specs + sealant application
  - Racking WITHOUT flashing: Photos showing properly applied sealant within/around attachment; sealant applied to allow water shedding while preventing decking/underlayment penetration
- **Validation:** Must follow manufacturer installation guidelines. Sealant must be visible and properly applied.

### R2 — Rail with Optimizer/Micro-inverters + EGC + Wire Management
- **Condition:** Always required; one photo per mounting plane
- **Required photo:** Pullback of each mounting plane showing:
  - All attachments visible
  - All rail visible
  - Solar product installed
  - Wire management using permanent, outdoor-rated, UV-resistant clips
  - Equipment Grounding Conductor (EGC) route showing copper grounding between rails
- **Validation:** Wires must be clipped/secured. EGC path must be traceable. Wires must be accessible for service.

### R3 — Complete Array(s) with Rail Trimmed
- **Condition:** Always required; one photo per array minimum
- **Required photo:** All modules visible in each array. Rail must be trimmed.
- **Special case:** Low-pitch roofs or tall homes — take photos from the roof to account for every panel
- **Validation:** Every panel must be visible. Rail must not extend past the last module.

### R4 — Under Array Wire Management
- **Condition:** Always required; one photo per array
- **Required photo:** Wire management for each array after panels are laid down
- **Validation:** Wires must NOT touch the roof surface. Wires/bundles must be secured and elevated above the surface to prevent damage.

### R5 — Tilt Measurement
- **Condition:** Always required; one photo per unique roof pitch
- **Required photo:** Tilt measurement with clearly legible readings
- **Critical rules:**
  - Measurement must be taken ON THE MODULE ITSELF, not on the roof surface
  - App screenshots acceptable ONLY if they show context of the array being measured
  - Screenshots of a tilt reading alone (without array context) are NOT acceptable
  - Multiple photos required for mixed pitch/surface roofs
- **Validation:** Reading must be legible. Module must be visible in frame.

### R6 — Rooftop Junction Box
- **Condition:** Always required; one photo per junction box
- **Required photo:** Open junction box with:
  - Completed wiring and bonding visible
  - Close-up of conductor terminated or transition through conduit
- **Critical rule:** Junction box must not have more than 2 inches of exposed conductors outside the array. More than 2" must be in conduit.
- **Validation:** Wiring must be complete and bonded. Exposed conductor length must be within spec.

## Roof Adders (If Applicable)

### AMS1 — Ground Mount System
- **Status:** INELIGIBLE from January 1, 2026 due to FEOC requirements. Do not accept ground mount jobs for Palmetto after this date.
- **Legacy requirements (pre-2026):** Structure photos (columns + rail attachment), stamped engineering plans with wind/snow calculations, embedment depths, footing dimensions

### AMS2 — Ballasted System
- **Condition:** If applicable
- **Required photos:**
  - Standard rooftop photo set (R1–R6)
  - Structure photos: roof pads, bays with and without weight blocks, wind deflectors, roof attachment clips
  - Ballast blocks must match ballast schedule
- **Required docs:** Stamped engineering plans with wind/snow calculations
- **Note:** If installation deviates from manufacturer recommendations (e.g., AHJ restrictions), include context on engineering plans

## Section 3: ELECTRICAL

### E1 — Inverter or Combiner Box (Enphase IQ / Q.Home)
- **Condition:** Enphase/QCells systems
- **Required photo:** Open combiner box showing complete AC wiring inside, including breakers from all branch circuits
- **Validation:** All branch circuit breakers must be visible. Box must be open.

### E2 — Main Breaker Rating
- **Condition:** Always required
- **Required photo:** Close-up of main breaker with rating clearly visible
- **Validation:** Amperage rating must be legible

### E3 — Main Panel Busbar Rating
- **Condition:** Always required
- **Required photo:** Panel label/sticker with busbar rating clearly visible
- **Validation:** Rating must be readable on the label

### E4 — Point of Interconnection (POI)
- **Condition:** Always required; 2 photos minimum
- **Required photos:**
  1. Contextual/pullback photo showing the full POI area
  2. Close-up showing insulated piercing connectors (IPCs), parallel lugs, distribution blocks, breakers, and wire terminations
- **Additional:** For Hoffman or load-side taps — photo of downstream sub panel with OCPD rating clearly visible
- **Validation:** Both photos required. All termination hardware must be visible in close-up.

### E5 — Pull Back of Balance of System (BOS)
- **Condition:** Always required
- **Required photo:** Full context pullback showing all BOS equipment installed
- **Validation:** All equipment must be visible in a single or multi-photo pullback

### E6 — Production Meter or CTs
- **Condition:** Always required
- **Standard requirement:** Photo of production meter or production CTs
- **RGM requirement:** Inverters must have internal revenue grade metering (RGM)
- **Enphase/QCells with micro-inverters/AC modules — additional requirements:**
  - Production CT showing visible L1 wiring from individual branch circuits passing through the single production CT in the IQ combiner box
  - Close-up of CT terminal lugs with correct CT phases in correct terminals
- **Validation:** CT wiring direction must be visible. Phasing must be correct and visible in photo.

### E7 — Consumption Metering (CTs)
- **Condition:** Always required on ALL projects
- **Required photo:** CTs on service feeders with direction of CTs visible
- **Exception:** If CTs physically cannot be installed (e.g., busbars in meter/main panels, split service setups), include photo context documenting why. These situations are rare.
- **Validation:** CT direction must be visible. If exception claimed, photo must clearly show why installation is impossible.

### E8 — Fused AC Disconnects
- **Condition:** Only if AC disconnects are fused
- **Required photo:** Completed wiring showing terminations, bonding, and clearly legible fuse ratings
- **Validation:** Fuse ratings must be readable. Wiring must be complete.

### E9 — Combiner Sub Panels
- **Condition:** Systems with multiple inverters, IQ Combiner boxes, or large sub panels
- **Required photo:** Each sub panel fully wired, with legible close-ups of breaker ratings and the panel's bus rating label
- **Validation:** All sub panels must be documented. Ratings must be readable.

## Section 4: STORAGE (If Battery Present)

### S1 — Battery Label
- **Condition:** Required IF monitoring portal access not granted at M1
- **Required photo:** Manufacturer label from the battery unit
- **Validation:** Make/model must be readable

### S2 — Comms Cable & Drain Wire
- **Condition:** Always required for battery installs
- **Required photo:** Both ends of comms cable terminated, showing:
  - Termination in every component
  - Drain wire visible and landed on ONE end only
- **Validation:** Both ends must be shown. Drain wire must be landed on exactly one end (not both, not neither).

### S3 — Battery CT
- **Condition:** Always required for battery installs
- **Required photo:** CT location and direction visible
- **Validation:** CT direction must be visible

### S4 — Pull Back of Battery BOS
- **Condition:** Always required for battery installs
- **Required photo:** Full context of battery install location relative to BOS equipment, with cover off showing compliance with manufacturer specs
- **Validation:** Cover must be off. Full install context visible.

## Storage Adders — Backup Battery (If Applicable)

### S5 — Gateway / System Controller / Transfer Switch Wiring
- **Condition:** Backup battery systems only
- **Required photo:** Transfer switch panel (Gateway) with all internal wiring and breaker ratings clearly legible
- **Validation:** All wiring visible. All ratings readable.

### S6 — Gateway / System Controller / Transfer Switch Label
- **Condition:** Backup battery systems + monitoring portal access not granted at M1
- **Required photo:** Manufacturer label showing make & model of ATS installed on site
- **Validation:** Make/model must be readable

---

## Section 5: SYSTEM COMMISSIONING

### SC1 — Commissioning Screenshots (Tesla Systems)
- **Condition:** Tesla systems only
- **Requirement:** System must be fully commissioned before leaving site
- **Required screenshots showing:**
  - 'LightReach' added as partner
  - Inverter & CTs enabled
  - Networking connected to Cellular
  - Operations settings page showing panel current limit & PCS settings
- **Validation:** All four data points must be visible across screenshots

### SC2 — Commissioning Screenshots (SolarEdge Systems)
- **Condition:** SolarEdge systems only
- **Required screenshots showing:**
  - 20% minimum backup reserve set
  - Storm Guard enabled
- **Validation:** Both settings must be visible and confirmed active

---

## Section 6: SITE IMPROVEMENTS / ADDERS

### SI1 — MPU / Trench / Tree Removal
- **Condition:** Submitted at Activation (M2), NOT at M1
- **Action:** Do not require this at M1. Flag for M2 submission instead.

### SI2 — Incentive State Serial Photos
- **Condition:** ALL incentive state projects, regardless of monitoring portal access
- **Required photo:** Clearly legible serial numbers for inverter (SolarEdge or other) or Enphase IQ combiner box serial
- **Note:** This duplicates PS2 — confirm both are captured for incentive states

## Validation Standards (Apply to All Photos)

1. **Legibility:** All serial numbers, ratings, labels, and model numbers must be clearly readable. Any blurry, dark, or partial shot fails.
2. **Context:** Many requirements call for both a pullback (context) AND a close-up. Both are required unless noted otherwise.
3. **Manufacturer specs:** Wire management, comms cables, and equipment installation must visibly follow manufacturer specifications.
4. **Conditional requirements:** Document the condition that triggers each optional requirement. If a battery is present, S1–S4 are always required.
5. **Per-array / per-unit counting:** R2, R3, R4, R6 require one photo per array/junction box respectively. A single photo does not cover multiple arrays.
6. **Photo authenticity:** Photos must be taken on-site during installation. Screenshots of tilt apps without array context are explicitly rejected.
